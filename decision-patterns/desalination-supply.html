Description

Provides a weather-independent water source but requires significant energy. Part of a diversified supply portfolio for water-scarce regions.

Typical Infrastructure

  • Reverse osmosis membrane systems
  • Intake and outfall structures
  • Energy recovery devices
  • Brine disposal systems

Monitoring Methods

  • Membrane performance sensors
  • Energy consumption tracking
  • Feed and product water quality monitoring
  • Environmental impact monitoring

Related Use Cases

  • Water Supply Security
  • Drought & Demand Management
