← Decision Patterns

Description

Stored water is released for treatment after the storm passes, preventing overflow into rivers and protecting water quality.

Typical Infrastructure

  • Deep tunnel boring machines
  • Interceptor sewers
  • Control chambers and drop shafts
  • Pumping stations
  • Connection to treatment works

Monitoring Methods

  • Flow sensors and tunnel level monitors
  • Overflow event detectors
  • SCADA systems
  • Structural integrity monitoring (fibre optic)

Related Use Cases

  • Sewage Overflow Monitoring
  • Urban Stormwater Management
