Description

Reduces demand on freshwater sources and improves supply resilience. NEWater in Singapore demonstrates potable reuse at national scale.

Typical Infrastructure

  • Advanced treatment (microfiltration, reverse osmosis, UV)
  • NEWater or similar reclamation facilities
  • Distribution pipelines for reclaimed water
  • Blending reservoirs

Monitoring Methods

  • Multi-parameter water quality sensors
  • Membrane integrity monitoring
  • Pathogen detection systems
  • Continuous process monitoring
