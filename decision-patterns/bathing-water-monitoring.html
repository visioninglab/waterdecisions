Description

Combines routine sampling with real-time prediction models. Results inform public advisory notices and regulatory classification of bathing waters.

Typical Infrastructure

  • Fixed sampling stations
  • Continuous water quality monitors
  • Weather stations
  • Public signage and notification apps

Monitoring Methods

  • E. coli and intestinal enterococci sampling
  • Turbidity and rainfall correlation models
  • Predictive algorithms
  • Public reporting dashboards
