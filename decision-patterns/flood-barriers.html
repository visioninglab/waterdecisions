Description

Includes tidal barriers, river walls, demountable defences, and natural levees. Selection depends on flood source, frequency, and the value of assets at risk.

Typical Infrastructure

  • Tidal barriers and flood gates
  • Flood walls and embankments
  • Demountable barriers
  • Sluice gates and natural levees

Monitoring Methods

  • River level gauges and tidal sensors
  • Weather radar
  • Flood forecasting models
  • Structural condition monitoring
