Approach

The six resilience principles — drawn from the UNDRR Handbook and ISO 22372 — remain the core analytical framework for every case study on this site.

The SDGs provide a supporting layer for policy translation, communication, filtering and summary views. Each principle maps indicatively to a set of SDG targets. These are areas of likely contribution, not rigid one-to-one assignments or formal certification.

Some case studies also connect to selected secondary SDGs — such as SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production), SDG 14 (Life Below Water) and SDG 15 (Life on Land) — where the evidence supports it.
